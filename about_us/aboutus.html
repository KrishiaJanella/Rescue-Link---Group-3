About Us

"At Rescue Link, we are dedicated to fostering disaster risk awareness and preparedness. Our mission is to establish a vital online resource hub, facilitating access to essential information, resources, and assistance for individuals and communities in times of crisis. Through our comprehensive platform, we empower people to safeguard themselves and their communities, effectively mitigating the effects of disasters and saving lives."







Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a beacon of hope and assistance in times of crisis. Through our Disaster Saver Website, we aim to empower individuals and communities by delivering timely information, resources, and support when they need it most. With a commitment to innovation and collaboration, we strive to be the trusted ally in navigating through disasters, offering resilience, relief, and recovery to those affected worldwide




Our Vision

Our goal in creating a disaster saving website is to provide a centralized platform where individuals and communities can access crucial information, resources, and support during emergencies. By offering a comprehensive hub for preparedness, response, and recovery efforts, we aim to empower people to protect themselves and their loved ones, ultimately minimizing the impact of disasters and saving lives.




Our Core Values

The core values of Rescue Link are rooted in compassion, empowering people to be proactive in their preparedness efforts and fostering a mindset of resilience. We believe in the power of collaboration, working closely with various stakeholders to innovate and improve the effectiveness of emergency services. Transparency, equity, and sustainability are at the heart of our approach, as we strive to provide timely, accurate information and advocate for long-term, holistic solutions that address the needs of all communities. Ultimately, our goal is to empower individuals and communities to take an active role in disaster management, while continuously improving our efforts based on lessons learned and emerging best practices.
