Landslide in Pulangbato, Cebu City threatens 175 residents

At least 175 individuals from a hinterland village here will be relocated to safer areas after a landslide struck near where they live. The Cebu City Government on Tuesday, February 28, 2023, confirmed that landslide hit portions of Sitio Riverside, Brgy. Pulangbato. The soil movement occurred last Monday evening, February 27, the City Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Office (CDRRMO) said. Fortunately, no one was reported hurt. They have also recorded no damages to infrastructure and properties. However, more than 100 residents near the landslide area might need to be transferred to safer places, authorities said.
